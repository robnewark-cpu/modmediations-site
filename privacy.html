Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

Mediation on Demand, LLC operates with an absolute commitment to client confidentiality, professional neutrality, and data integrity.

1. Information Collection

We do not use invasive tracking pixels or marketing cookies. Information is strictly gathered through voluntary client submissions via our secure Case Intake and Panel Registration forms to process alternative dispute requests.

2. Technical Telemetry

This platform utilizes privacy-first network optimization tools (including Cloudflare RUM) to monitor anonymous technical rendering speeds and ensure edge network stability. No personally identifiable data is harvested or tracked during this process.

3. Non-Disclosure

All dispute parameters, case notes, and institutional data files remain strictly confidential and are never disclosed, rented, or distributed to outside networks without explicit legal authorization.
