Robert Newark, Esq.

Principal Mediator & Managing Director

Robert Newark is an accomplished executive attorney, corporate CEO, and former state agency counsel with nearly two decades of comprehensive experience navigating complex regulatory architectures, administering dispute resolution frameworks, and conducting high-consequence independent investigations. Known for his objective demeanor and analytical clarity, Robert has spent his career serving as a trusted, neutral advisor to senior executives, judicial officers, and high-level institutional stakeholders.

As the Chief Executive Officer and Managing Partner of a regional professional services enterprise, Robert directs internal labor and employment compliance, adjudicates complex workplace grievances, and resolves multi-jurisdictional operational disputes across distributed footprints spanning six distinct state jurisdictions (TX, OK, NM, CO, AR). His background includes deep expertise in Commercial & Business conflicts, workplace liability triage, and independent corporate fact-finding. Additionally, Robert brings specialized proficiency to the technology sector, having spearheaded the complete structural modernization of cross-state enterprise infrastructure into a secured, zero-trust cloud network to guarantee absolute information integrity and confidentiality.

Prior to his executive corporate tenure, Robert served as Assistant General Counsel for the Oklahoma Employment Security Commission. In this role, he commanded high-volume state administrative enforcement and labor portfolios, interpreting complex state and federal labor laws while providing expert, neutral advice to administrative law judges and agency executives. His re-engineering of the agency's compliance frameworks successfully optimized dispute timelines, resulting in a 50% compression of outstanding administrative liabilities.

Education & Credentials

  • Juris Doctor (J.D.) — Oklahoma City University School of Law
  • Active Member in Good Standing — Supreme Court of Texas & Oklahoma Bar Association
  • Admitted to practice before the U.S. 5th and 10th Circuit Court of Appeals
  • Honorable Discharge & 100% Service-Connected Disabled Veteran — United States Army
